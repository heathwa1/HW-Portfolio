A little about me...

Strength, determination, and just plain stubborness keeps me moving forward in the accumulation of knowledge.

I love a good challenge. When I decided to transition careers from service industry to urban development it was a move that I was determined to succeed in. What I loved about the service industry was the opportunity to personalize experiences and create memorable moments for guests who chose to visit. Listening to their stories and paying attention to details showed them that I cared about what I did and about them. Making people smile and brightening their day was always the goal. It turns out that urban development is quite similar in that regard. The ability to focus on details and promote changes by listening to what people say is a powerful tool for success. Through careful listening and observation, data driven analysis can be meaningful in the imagination of the growth of space.
